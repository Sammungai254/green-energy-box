Who We Are

The Green Energy Box – Sustainable Co-generation Solutions

The Green Energy Box is a Co-generation Combined Heat and Power (CHP) plant solution for the simultaneous production of electricity and process heat.

  • Available in two versions: Biomass-fired and Concentrated Solar Power (CSP).
  • Produces three outputs: electricity, heat for thermal processes, and cooling solutions.
  • Offered under innovative concepts: Energy as a Service (EAAS) and Cooling as a Service (CAAS).
